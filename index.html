My teaching philosophy

What I believe about children

My practice is grounded in real theory and a whole lot of heart. Here is what guides every plan I write and every interaction I have.

“

I see children as capable, curious and full of ideas. When we follow their interests, build warm relationships, and create space to play, children show us just how much they can do.

— Maxine's guiding belief
🧭

Child-led & playful

Children's interests, ideas and voices guide the learning. They build understanding through play and exploration, inspired by Reggio Emilia, Montessori and Piaget.

🤝

Relationships first

Children learn best when they feel safe, valued and connected. Secure, responsive relationships create a real sense of belonging (Vygotsky, EYLF Principle 1).

🌍

Every culture a strength

Each child's language, family and identity is woven into the program as something to celebrate, never something to overlook.

💗

Learning to care

I help children name feelings, share, and repair after conflict, treating every moment as a chance to grow kind, capable little people.
